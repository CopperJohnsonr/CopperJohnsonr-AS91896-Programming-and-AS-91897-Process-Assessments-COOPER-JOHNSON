WORKOUT HELPER HELP

Hello and welcome to the Help File!
If you are having trouble navigating this file, follow the following instructions:

1. Login using your username and password.

2. Create an account if you are a new user.

3. Select Find Exercises.

4. Enter:
- Arms
- Legs
- Chest
- Core
- Back
Or press the equivalent muscle on the diagram.

5. Click Search.

6. Workout plans are available from the Home screen.

7. View the usage graph to see how often the program has been opened.

8. Change your password from the Home screen.

9. Click the muscle images to quickly view exercises.
